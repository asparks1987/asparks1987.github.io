# Site Positioning Source of Truth

## Primary Audience

The site should speak first to:

- MSPs
- WISPs
- IT departments
- Network operators
- Infrastructure-heavy small and midsize businesses
- Businesses with spreadsheet-driven or manual operational workflows
- Companies needing custom internal software, automation, monitoring, or reporting

The site should not imply Aryn only works with those industries. They are the strongest initial specialization.

## Positioning Statement

Aryn Sparks designs and builds operational software for businesses that depend on networks, data, and repeatable workflows.

Supporting description:

Independent software consultant and network engineer specializing in network monitoring, automation, internal business applications, operational dashboards, data systems, and infrastructure-aware software.

## Primary Services

1. Network Monitoring and Automation
   - Helps businesses gain visibility into infrastructure, identify failures earlier, reduce repetitive IT work, and make network operations easier to manage.
   - Representative deliverables: device and service monitoring, outage and performance alerts, network-health dashboards, automated checks, device inventory, topology visibility, reporting, platform integrations, and custom MSP/WISP/IT operations tools.

2. Custom Internal Business Applications
   - Replaces spreadsheet-heavy, email-driven, fragmented, or highly manual business processes with purpose-built software.
   - Representative deliverables: staff portals, customer portals, job/ticket/inventory/asset tracking, request workflows, scheduling tools, database-backed apps, API integrations, role-based admin interfaces, and back-office automation.

3. Business Dashboards and Reporting
   - Turns data from spreadsheets, databases, APIs, monitoring platforms, and business systems into understandable operational views.
   - Representative deliverables: executive dashboards, service-health dashboards, operations reports, recurring automated reports, KPI trends, filtered/exportable reports, and data cleanup.

Machine learning, mobile development, integrations, and advanced analytics are supporting capabilities. Do not present neural networking as a standalone primary service.

## Primary CTA

Book a discovery call.

Closing CTA language:

Tell me what is slowing down your network, operations, or internal workflow.

## Approved Proof-Project Claims

LAN Scanner Pro:

- Publicly released Android network-scanning application.
- Local-first network discovery.
- Device history and topology-related workflows.
- Reporting and operator-focused tooling.
- Proof that Aryn can finish and release a real product.

NOCWALL-CE:

- Private alpha network-operations platform.
- Work includes wallboards, telemetry, topology, edge-agent concepts, alerts, APIs, and deployment tooling.
- Proof of full-stack network-platform engineering.

AlphaRunner:

- Anonymized commissioned analytics web application.
- Includes FastAPI application work, scheduled workers, data pipelines, authentication, dashboards, databases, and deployment automation.
- Should remain framed as research-only or decision-support software. Do not provide financial advice language.

Open Source and R&D:

- Evidence of reusable library design, packaging, documentation, testing, deterministic behavior, developer tooling, networking knowledge, and public follow-through.
- Research/passion projects may include OneOS / OS_One, HOME-A.I., and ProjectTDIR, but they must remain secondary to the consulting message.

## Prohibited Or Unverified Claims

Do not invent or imply:

- Testimonials or customer quotations.
- ROI percentages.
- Revenue.
- User counts.
- Download counts.
- Performance results.
- Uptime claims.
- Security certifications.
- Partnerships.
- Client names.
- Production maturity for alpha projects.
- Awards.
- Years of experience.
- Team members.
- Investment-performance claims or financial advice.

Do not imply private repositories are open source.

Differentiate clearly between public release, private alpha, commissioned client work, deployed system, open-source library, and research or passion project.
